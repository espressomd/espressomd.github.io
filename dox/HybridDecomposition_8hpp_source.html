48 /**
49  * @brief Hybrid decomposition cell system.
50  *
51  * Store particles with short-range interactions
52  * in a @ref RegularDecomposition cell system and
53  * particles with long-range interactions
54  * in a @ref AtomDecomposition (N-square) cell system.
55  * All regular cells are coupled to the N-square cells.
56  */

66 
67  /** RegularDecomposition to hold the small particles */
