22 /**
23  * @file
24  * MMM1D algorithm for long-range %Coulomb interactions on the CPU.
25  * Implementation of the MMM1D method for the calculation of the electrostatic
26  * interaction in one-dimensionally periodic systems. For details on the
27  * method see MMM in general. The MMM1D method works only with the N-squared
28  * cell system since neither the near nor far formula can be decomposed.
29  */
