Detailed Description

Reaction ensemble method.

Works for the reaction ensemble at constant volume and temperature. For the reaction ensemble at constant pressure, additionally employ a barostat! NOTE: a chemical reaction consists of a forward and backward reaction. Here both reactions have to be defined separately. The extent of the reaction is here chosen to be +1. If the reaction trial move for a dissociation of HA is accepted then there is one more dissociated ion pair H+ and A-. Implementation of [44].
