31  * @brief CUDA implementation of @ref SystemInterface.
32  *
33  * When data is synchronized between host and device memory, a subset
34  * of the @ref Particle struct is copied from each particle on the host
35  * to the corresponding @ref CUDA_particle_data struct on the device via
36  * @ref EspressoSystemInterface::gatherParticles(). Once the transfer is
37  * complete, the particle AoS on the device is copied (or "split") to
